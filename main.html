Paris, France


Paris is known as the City of Light and is famous for its rich history, beautiful architecture, art museums, and romantic atmosphere. It attracts millions of visitors every year from all over the world.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris
  2. Louvre Museum – home to world-famous artworks
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a historic Gothic masterpiece
  4. Champs-Élysées – a famous avenue for shopping and sightseeing
  5. Montmartre – known for its artistic history and views
  • Croissants – a classic French breakfast
  • Crêpes – sweet or savory street food
  • Macarons – colorful and delicious desserts
  • French Cheese – rich and flavorful varieties
  • Baguette – a staple of French cuisine
