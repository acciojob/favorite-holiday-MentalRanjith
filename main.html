Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light," is renowned for its art, culture, and iconic landmarks. From charming streets to world-class museums, it offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.

  • Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris offering stunning views.
  • Louvre Museum – Home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located on Montmartre hill with panoramic city views.
  • Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue known for shopping and cafes.
    • Croissants – Flaky and buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
    • Baguettes – Traditional French bread with a crisp crust.
    • Escargots – Snails cooked in garlic butter, a classic delicacy.
    • Crème Brûlée – A rich dessert with a caramelized top.
    • Macarons – Colorful and sweet almond-based cookies.
